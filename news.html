Barcodes expand range of high-resolution sensor

April 25, 2024 — Shawn Ballard

WashU researchers developed an optical whispering-gallery-mode barcode technique that tracks multiple resonant modes simultaneously, expanding sensor dynamic range and improving accuracy for biomedical and environmental measurement.

The story highlights a new IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement paper on multimode WGM barcodes and notes NIH support for the research.
