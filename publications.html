Scientific Contributions

Multimode barcode sensing

Developed optical WGM barcode readout that extends dynamic range and improves high-resolution physical and chemical sensing.

Robust resonator packaging

Designed PDMS-encapsulated and hydrogel-integrated WGM sensors for stable displacement and biochemical measurements.

Physics-aware instrument design

Combined resonator optics with signal processing theory to deliver interpretable, high-sensitivity measurements in realistic environments.
